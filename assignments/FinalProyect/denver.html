History

The Denver Colorado Temple is the 40th operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The LDS Church announced plans to build a temple in Colorado on March 31, 1982. Almost two years later, Gordon B. Hinckley presided over the site dedication and groundbreaking
